Welcome to Articulate Toronto

Articulate Toronto's Gavel Club (ATGC)

Who We Are

ATGC is a chapter of Toastmasters International, aspiring to provide more youth with the opportunity to participate in public speaking events. This initiative aims to help youth with improving their entrepreneurship and leadership skills.

Our Mission

We aim to help youth become global leaders in subjects such as law, politics, business and social justice as well as improve their entrepreneurship and leadership.

Our Plan

Our bi-weekly meetings aim to innovate creativity, teach valuable skills and inspire youth of all ages with a unique and enriched learning environment. ATGC meetings are host to a variety of activities such as debate, improv. and prepared speeches, general problem solving conversations and much more.

Each meeting will also have dedicated time for members to engage in entrepreneurial endeavours where all profits go to a charity of choice.

To bring in valuable real-life experience, ATGC also hosts guest speakers and industry professionals every other month who help provide additional help with career-related skills such as resume building.

Location

The Artscape Youngplace

Situated on 180 Shaw Street, the Artscape Youngplace, where ATGC's meetings take place, is a community cultural hub devoted to artistic creation, learning and collaboration. For more information regarding our venue, visit the Artscape Youngplace website.
